Ingredients:
 - 500g bread flour
 - 10g salt
 - 10g fresh yeast
 - 350g water

Directions:
 1. Mix and knead dough.

 2. Prove for a couple of hours.

 3. Fold and shape dough.

 4. Preheat oven to 250 C.

 3. Prove for an hour or so and then bake for about 25 minutes.

 5. Wait to cool and the eat with butter - it's lovely.
